B Vocabulary – Family Words

💡 Tip: We say my/his wife (singular) but our/their wives (plural).
husband
a man who is married
Ivan is Anne's husband.
wife
a woman who is married
Anne is Ivan's wife.
parents
father and mother together
Ivan and Anne are Karen's parents.
father
a male parent
Ivan is Karen's father.
mother
a female parent
Anne is Jack's mother.
son
a male child
Jack is their son.
daughter
a female child
Karen is their daughter.
brother
a male sibling
Jack is Karen's brother.
sister
a female sibling
Karen is Jack's sister.
grandfather
father's/mother's father
Henry is Karen's grandfather.
grandmother
father's/mother's mother
Diana is Jack's grandmother.
grandson
son's/daughter's son
Jack is Henry's grandson.
granddaughter
son's/daughter's daughter
Karen is Diana's granddaughter.
uncle
parent's brother
George is Karen's uncle.
aunt
parent's sister
Amelia is Jack's aunt.
nephew
brother's/sister's son
Jack is George's nephew.
niece
brother's/sister's daughter
Karen is Meena's niece.
cousin
uncle's/aunt's child
Ravi is Karen's cousin.
only child
no brothers or sisters
"I am an only child."
relatives
all members of a family
They are all relatives.
