💍 Unit 2 – Birth, Marriage and Death

English Vocabulary in Use · Elementary

A Birth

Key phrases

Anna had a baby yesterday.
He was born at 1.15.
He weighed 3 kilograms.
They called him John after his grandfather.
His grandfather's birthday is June 16th.

Was born

He was born in 1957.
The parents were born in 1986.

Used for the year, place, or date someone came into the world.

⚠️ Common mistakes:
✅ Anna had a baby  |  ❌ Anna got a baby
✅ He was born  |  ❌ He born / He is born

B Marriage

Status words

Single – no partner
Married – has husband/wife
Widowed – husband/wife died
Separated – marriage breaking up
Divorced – marriage legally ended

Key phrases

Harry and Sarah got married.
They married in 2001. (formal)
They went on their honeymoon.
They were married for 15 years.

bride = woman at wedding
groom = man at wedding

⚠️ Common mistake:
✅ Sarah got married to Harry  |  ❌ married with Harry

C Death

Key phrases

Harry became ill.
He died last year.
He died of a heart attack.
The funeral was on Monday.

died / dead / death

He died last year. (verb)
He is dead. (adjective)
His death was sudden. (noun)

⚠️ Common mistake:
✅ Harry is dead  |  ❌ Harry is died / Harry is death
